About Dhami Electronics

Your trusted partner for premium electronics in Nepal since 2026

Our Story

Dhami Electronics was started in 2026 by me, Dinesh Singh Dhami, with a vision to build a reliable and affordable electronics platform in Nepal. As a student of computer engineering, I wanted to create something practical that solves real problems and makes technology more accessible.

This is currently a startup project in its early stage, built with passion, learning, and experimentation. The goal is to provide useful electronic products with transparent pricing and a simple online experience. I'm personally involved in designing, developing, and improving every part of this platform.

Dhami Electronics is not a large company yet — it is a growing idea. With time, effort, and customer trust, I aim to expand it into a trusted destination for electronics across Nepal.

Our Mission

The goal of Dhami Electronics is to make genuine and useful electronics more accessible to people in Nepal through a simple and transparent online platform. As a startup, the focus is on learning, improving, and building trust by offering carefully selected products at fair prices.

This project is also a step toward connecting modern technology with everyday needs, while continuously improving the experience based on real user feedback.
